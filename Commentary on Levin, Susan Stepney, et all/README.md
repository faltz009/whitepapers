## Summary
This commentary positions the whitepaper series in dialogue with Stepney et al. on time, self-reference, and living systems.

Core points:
- argues convergence between independently developed frameworks,
- emphasizes self-reference as productive and time-extended,
- proposes constraint-first approaches for vast design spaces,
- outlines practical bridges for modeling self-modifying open systems.

## Role in the Series
